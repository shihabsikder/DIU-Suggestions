Become a Contributor

Want to help your classmates? Contribute your question papers and notes to DIU Suggestions!

Benefits of Contributing:

  • Recognition: Get featured as a contributor on our platform
  • Community Impact: Help hundreds of students prepare for exams
  • Portfolio Building: Showcase your contributions to universities and employers
  • Academic Growth: Reinforce your understanding by teaching others

How to Contribute:

  1. Click the "Upload Question" button on the Suggestions page
  2. Select the course, department, exam type, and semester
  3. Upload the question paper (PDF format)
  4. Add any additional details or notes
  5. Submit for review
